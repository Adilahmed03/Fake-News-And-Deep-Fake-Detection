ℹ️ How it works

Our deepfake detection system uses a combination of ResNext CNN for feature extraction and LSTM networks for temporal analysis across video frames.

Model: ResNext + LSTM
Accuracy: Up to 93.6% (100 frames)
Processing: Analyzes 20 frames from your video

⚠️ Note

The current implementation uses a placeholder model for demonstration. For production use, integrate the full ResNext+LSTM model from the Deepfake_detection_using_deep_learning project.
